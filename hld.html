General Summary: Somewhere in some world of ones and zeroes there was a thing that lived and did jobs and stuff! But then it got bored because it’s friends stopped getting inputs to operate on and so they didn’t know what to do. but then a new thing came and it was super weird! So they chcked it out and OHNO SOMEONE’S IN TROUBLE but they can’t get inside the thing so they operate the other things remotely to help them and stuff!

Meanwhile the other person in the other thing is in danger because weird robots and machines and lasers and THAT BADGUY OF DARK NASTY BADNESS also explosions.

Anyway the characters in the digital world have more binary switches and puzzle solving and top-down manuipulation of stuff with multiple characters at once. Then in the other world things are a side-view platformery thing with stuff manpiulated from that other world. Or. Something.

Help the other person get safe from BADGUY in time and unlock sectors for THE WORLD and try to unlock the barrier between both worlds! But don’t let the signal get low and don’t die! Dying is bad!

Gameplay:

  • Top-down puzzle solving. Partially pushblock puzzles, some micromanging multiple characters in multiple places at once. They activate and control things on the other side. Some characters do things in different ways than the others.
  • Side view action platforming. Jump jump jump! Or dig! Also claw! Claws can destroy things but once something is destroyed it can not be undestroyed. And BADGUY has armor and will catch the weirdstranger if they get close. Don’t try that! But it can work to get through obstacles. Mind the timer.
  • Switching: While both can be viewed at the same time, only one can be controlled by the player at a time. Left to her own devices, the other character will mostly stay in the same place or dig. The other character left to their own devices will usually banter with eachother or just stay in place if they’re already operating a device/interface/switch. Sometimes they might wander from side to side.
  • Chars:
  • Gold (First player puzzle solver thingy. Does everything okay.)
  • Pink (Fast use of machines/switches. Also can make some things.)
  • Red (Moves fast. Not reliable for machines but works fine with switches.)
  • Dog: Can jump and dig and stuff! Doesn’t really know how to robot but can listen to instructions! ...but also can’t read. Maybe.
  • BAD GUY: is bad and feels bad! Do not confront!!!1
  • Devices:
  • Laser Sentry
  • Plasma Sentry
  • Allchars
  • Cleanerbot. Cleans up stuff and moves along walls.
  • Door/Directory: Goes to a place that isn’t here. Not always open/active.
  • Grabber: Drops down and picks up stuff. Sometimes crushes stuff.
  • Bubble: It’s a bubble. It explodes. It’s easy to manufacture.
  • Terminal: Facilitates communications between both realms. Turning these on activates stuff! Also maybe connect things to them for good reasons!
  • Fan: It blows things around. Including chars.
  • Bin: Stuff goes in it. You probably don’t want to get in it.
  • Crushything: Don’t get inside. But it can crush stuff!
